StayRwanda.com — Full Website Documentation (Version 2.0)

1. Executive Summary

Project Name: StayRwanda
Domain: StayRwanda.com
Business Type: Property booking & rental marketplace
Region: Rwanda (starting with Kigali, later nationwide)

Overview

StayRwanda is a digital marketplace connecting guests and property owners across Rwanda. The platform enables users to search, compare, book, and manage apartments, vacation rentals, guesthouses, and hotels.

StayRwanda combines:

* Apartment rentals
* Short stays
* Hotel booking
* Long-term leasing
* Property management

Unlike global competitors, StayRwanda is designed specifically for the Rwandan market.
